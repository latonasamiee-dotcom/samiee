About

Where precision
meets imagination

I am a multidisciplinary design professional with over 7 years of hands-on experience spanning the full spectrum of architectural practice and 3D digital production. My work covers everything from initial brief and schematic concept through to BIM-coordinated technical documentation, planning permit submissions, and construction oversight on one side — and character modelling, rigging, game trailer direction, and cinematic product animation on the other.

What makes my work distinctive is the cross-pollination between disciplines. My architectural training sharpens every 3D scene I produce: spatial intuition, disciplined lighting, and an obsessive eye for proportion that no amount of software tutorials can replicate. Conversely, my command of real-time engines and cinematic tools elevates every architectural presentation I deliver far beyond what a traditional practice can offer.

I have worked remotely with clients across Nigeria, the United States, Turkey, and Saudi Arabia, consistently delivering to broadcast and publication quality under real production pressures. I am a disciplined self-starter and a committed creative who treats every project as an opportunity to raise the standard.
